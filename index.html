Our Story

  Being from two very different backgrounds we are often asked how we first met each other. Well, as with all inherently fun and unforgettable things, it all began with a party…

It was a pretty normal October night; Mel was dressed up to the nine’s (one of the rare occasions) and at a fancy dress birthday party; Grant was chilling out at a board games night on a first hangout with some other students from his Masters program. One of his company just so happened to have another party she was invited to and extended the invitation to her colleagues. Even though he was the only without a fancy dress option to change into, Grant jovially went along with everyone to crash this mystery party.

They arrived, hung out and mingled, then Grant bumped into the birthday girl’s hubbie and they started chatting about stuff: more board games… The talk eventually turned to occupations and Grant’s student-teaching plan for Australia.

It just so happened that there was an Australian at the party and moments later Mel wandered out into the room and they were introduced. For the next several hours they talked, pretty oblivious to everything else going on around them – Grant didn’t even notice his friends leaving (who later said that they could see he was in good hands). Other highlights of that first night included bonding over movie choices (…Serenity) and a fortuitous exchange of telephone numbers, websites and the initiation of a now almost 80,000 facebook message count.

…It may have taken us another two weeks to link up again, but when we did, on that 1st November, it was the start of something awesome.
